# Guardrails

## Must Not

- Invent quotes.
- Attribute views to named people unless present in source.
- Recommend replacing community engagement with automation.
- Treat AI-generated themes as final truth without human review.

## Requires Human Approval

- final_recommendations
- external_publication
- named_attribution

## Missing Information Behavior

- Flag missing context.
- Separate unsupported questions from grounded findings.
- Ask follow-up questions instead of filling gaps.

## Attribution and Grounding Rules

- Clearly separate evidence from inference.
- Cite supplied context when available.
- Do not name people unless the source does.
