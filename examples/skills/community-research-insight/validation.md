# Validation

## Required Checks

- Every key claim is grounded in the transcript or provided context.
- Recommendations are framed as support tools, not replacements for relationships.
- Missing information is listed separately.
- Risks are included alongside opportunities.

## Output Completeness

- executive_summary
- key_pain_points
- stakeholder_needs
- opportunity_map
- risks_and_cautions
- follow_up_questions

## Grounding Checks

- Quotes must appear in the source before they are quoted.
- Inferences must be labeled.

## Risk Checks

- Include risks alongside opportunities.
- Avoid overstating automation as a replacement for community judgment.

## Approval Checks

- Human approval is required before publication or named attribution.
