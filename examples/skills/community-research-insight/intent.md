# Intent

## Outcome

Produce a structured insight brief that helps researchers understand key issues and identify practical support opportunities without replacing community judgment.

## Users

- Community-engaged researchers
- Program staff
- Technology support partners

## Required Inputs

- transcript_or_notes

## Expected Outputs

- executive_summary
- key_pain_points
- stakeholder_needs
- opportunity_map
- risks_and_cautions
- follow_up_questions

## Constraints

- Do not invent quotes.
- Clearly separate evidence from inference.
- Treat the output as a draft for human review.

## Success Scenarios

- The brief identifies grounded themes and practical support opportunities.
- Risks and missing context are visible.

## Failure Scenarios

- The source material is too thin to support conclusions.
- Named attribution is requested but not supported by the transcript.
- The output overstates what automation should replace.
