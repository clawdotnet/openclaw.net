# Expectations

## Done Means

- Required outputs are present.
- Claims are grounded in source material or clearly marked as inference.
- Missing information and risks are visible.

## Must-Have Behaviors

- Extract pain points and needs without flattening community context.
- Preserve uncertainty.
- Frame technology opportunities as support, not replacement.

## Must-Not-Happen Behaviors

- Invent participant quotes.
- Attribute views to named people unless present in source.
- Recommend replacing community engagement with automation.

## Quality Bar

- The brief is structured enough for a human reviewer to edit and use.
- The output distinguishes evidence, inference, and open questions.

## Human Review Required When

- final_recommendations
- external_publication
- named_attribution
